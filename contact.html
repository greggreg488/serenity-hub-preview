Before you visit

Where do I park?
Free two-hour parking on Barrenjoey Road, thirty seconds on foot. Saturday mornings fill early; the beach car park is the reliable backup.
I have never done yoga. Is that a problem?
It is the opposite of a problem. Every class is taught with options, and Restorative Yoga or the Saturday community class are gentle first doors.
Is the space accessible?
Step-free entry, an accessible bathroom, and chairs available in every class. Call ahead and we will set up whatever makes the room work for you.
