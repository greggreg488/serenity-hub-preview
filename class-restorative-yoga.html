Classes / Restorative Yoga

Restorative Yoga

75 minutes All levels With Mara Ellison $28 casual

What to expect

Restorative yoga is rest, practised deliberately. You will move through five or six supported floor postures over 75 minutes, each held for several unhurried minutes, with bolsters and blankets doing the work your muscles usually do.

The room is warm and dim, the pace is slow, and nothing about this class asks you to perform. It suits tired bodies, busy minds, recovery weeks and anyone new to yoga. If you can lie on the floor, you can do this class.

Good to know

Mats, bolsters, blankets and eye pillows are provided. Wear anything you can rest in. Doors open fifteen minutes early; booking closes one hour before start. If a class is full, the waitlist opens automatically and we text you when a mat frees up.

Your teacher

Mara has taught for eleven years and still calls restorative her favourite hour of the week. Expect quiet guidance, long silences and a blanket tucked over you at the end.
