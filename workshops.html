How workshops run

Groups stay small, usually eight to ten people, so there is time for your questions and your mistakes. All materials, tools and aprons are included in the price, along with tea and something baked.

Whatever you make goes home with you, fired, cured or knotted. If a session sells out, join the waitlist and we will let you know the moment a bench opens up.

Give a workshop, not a thing

Gift cards cover any workshop or class, and they never rush anyone. Find them in the shop.
