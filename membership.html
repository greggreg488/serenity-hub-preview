Fair questions

Can I pause my membership?
Yes, for up to eight weeks a year, from your dashboard, no phone call required. Travel, injury, newborns and busy seasons are all real life.
Is there a minimum term?
No. Weekly billing, cancel whenever you like, and we will still wave at you at the beach.
Can I bring a friend?
Unlimited members get two guest passes each season. Everyone can bring a friend to the Saturday community class, which is pay what you can.
How do the two intro weeks work?
$30 flat buys fourteen days of unlimited classes so you can find your teachers and your time slots. It rolls into a membership only if you say so.

Start with the two intro weeks

Fourteen days, every class, $30. The habit will introduce itself.
